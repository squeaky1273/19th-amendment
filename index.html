What is the 19th Amendment?

The 19th amendment was the amendment in charge of guarantying women the right to vote in the elections, this was a huge leap within women movements in America

How did it started?

It all started with the Sufrage Movement, many women of color were discriminated

The Sufrage Movement started in New York and it was a big attraction for a wide range of women, even though a lot of women were attracted to this movement they usually excluded colored women.
